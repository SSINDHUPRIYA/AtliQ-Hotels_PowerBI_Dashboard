## 📊 AtliQ Hotels — Revenue & Occupancy Analytics Dashboard

This project analyzes hotel performance data for AtliQ Hotels, a
hospitality chain operating across multiple cities in India. Using
SQL for data extraction and transformation, and Power BI for
visualization, this dashboard provides a comprehensive view of
revenue, occupancy, and pricing metrics to support strategic
decision-making.

### 🎯 Objective
To help AtliQ Hotels' management identify revenue trends, occupancy
patterns, and pricing opportunities across cities, properties, and
booking platforms — enabling smarter, data-driven business decisions.

### 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- **SQL** — Data cleaning, transformation, and aggregation
- **Power BI** — Interactive dashboard, DAX measures, KPI visualization
- **DAX** — Custom calculations for RevPAR, ADR, Realisation %

### 📈 Key Metrics Tracked
- Total Revenue, RevPAR, ADR, Occupancy %, Realisation %
- DSRN (Daily Sellable Room Nights), DBRN (Daily Booked Room Nights)
- Weekday vs Weekend performance comparison

### 🔍 Key Insights
- Atliq Blu and Atliq City lead in occupancy rate and guest ratings
- Business category contributes 61.6% of total revenue vs 38.4%
  from Luxury
- Direct Online and LogTrip booking platforms show the highest
  realisation percentage
- Significant RevPAR variance between weekday and weekend bookings
  highlights dynamic pricing opportunities

### 📁 Dashboard Features
- City, Room Class, and Date range filters
- Property-level performance table with sorting
- Pricing strategy trend analysis by week
- Revenue category breakdown (Luxury vs Business)
- Booking platform performance comparison

### 💡 What I Learned
Working on this project helped me understand how to model real-world
hospitality data, design meaningful KPIs from business requirements,
and build dashboards that don't just display data — but tell a clear,
actionable story for decision-makers.
